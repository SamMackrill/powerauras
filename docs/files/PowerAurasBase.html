PowaAuras:RotateCoordPair (x, y, ox, oy, a, asp)
Used for rotating a pair of coordinates by a given amount of radians about any origin point with any aspect ratio.

Parameters

  • x: The X coordinate of the pair to rotate.
  • y: The Y coordinate of the pair to rotate.
  • ox: The origin X coordinate of the texture, as a float. 0.5 represents the center of the image.
  • oy: The origin Y coordinate of the texture, as a float. 0.5 represents the center of the image.
  • a: The rotation to be applied in radians. Use math.rad to convert degrees to radians.
  • asp: The aspect ratio of the image.

Return value:

An X and Y coordinate pair to be used as a corner in a SetTexCoord call.

See also:

  • http://www.wowpedia.org/SetTexCoord_Transformations#Rotation_of_textures_about_any_point_with_any_aspect
PowaAuras:RotateTexture (texture, deg, ULx, ULy, LLx, LLy, URx, URy, LRx, LRy)
Rotates the given texture around the centre by a certain amount of degrees.

Parameters

  • texture: The texture object to modify.
  • deg: The amount of rotation to apply in degrees. Positive values rotate clockwise.
  • ULx: The top left corner X coordinate of the texture segment to be rotated.
  • ULy: The top left corner Y coordinate of the texture segment to be rotated.
  • LLx: The bottom left corner X coordinate of the texture segment to be rotated.
  • LLy: The bottom left corner Y coordinate of the texture segment to be rotated.
  • URx: The top right corner X coordinate of the texture segment to be rotated.
  • URy: The top right corner Y coordinate of the texture segment to be rotated.
  • LRx: The bottom right corner X coordinate of the texture segment to be rotated.
  • LRy: The bottom right corner Y coordinate of the texture segment to be rotated.
